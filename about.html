About Enlight

Enlight is the student organization that programs and maintains the Máquina fountain on the UW-Madison campus. We also work on various other hardware and software projects to expand and apply our knowledge, encompassing everything from Python scripts to microcontrollers. We welcome aspiring engineers of any skill level to help contribute!

About Enlight

Mission Statement

Enlight has the following aims:

  1. Provide a friendly environment for students to learn about computer hardware and software and share this knowledge with peers.
  2. To engage students in hands-on, real-world projects that they would not experience inside the classroom.
  3. To connect students with outside companies to form lasting relationships.
  4. To maintain the Máquina Fountain so it may continue to serve as an iconic symbol of the College of Engineering and UW-Madison.
